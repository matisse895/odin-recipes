Quinoa Salad

Recipe Description

Our favorite thing to eat when we want to feel healthy.

Ingredients

  • Two bell peppers, diced
  • One yellow onion, diced
  • Three baby cucumbers (or one large english cucumber), diced
  • One bunch of cilantro, chopped
  • One bunch of parsely, chopped
  • Two avocados, diced
  • One cup quinoa, cooked according to package directions
  • The juice of two lemons
  • Half a cup of hummus
  • 1/4C olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Process

  1. Mix vegetables, herbs, and quinoa in large bowl.
  2. Combine hummus, oil, and lemon juice in seperate bowl.
  3. Season hummus dressing with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and any other seasonings you like, to taste.
  4. Drizzle dressing over salad and mix thoroughly.
