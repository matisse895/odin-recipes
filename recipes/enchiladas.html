American Enchiladas

Recipe Description

My Mother-in-law's friend gave us this bastardized tex-mex enchilada recipe and now it's one of our favorites

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • One rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Coriander
  • Chili Powder
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 jar of salsa or picante sauce
  • 3 cups (1 bag) shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • salt, to taste

Processes

  1. Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees F.

  2. Season Chicken

    Put shredded chicken in bowl.Season with cumin, coriander, oregano, chili powder, and salt, to taste.

  3. Make Sauce

    In a seperate bowl, dump cream of mushroom soup and jar of salsa. Mix well.

  4. Assemble Enchiladas

    In a 9x11 baking dish, spread a thin layer of sauce.
    In a tortilla, roll up chicken, cheese, and a bit of sauce and place rolled up tortilla in pan, seam side down. Repeat until dish is full.
    Dump remaining sauce on top of enchiladas. Top with remaining cheese. Bake at 375 for 30 minutes.
    Remove foil to brown top for 10 more minutes. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
